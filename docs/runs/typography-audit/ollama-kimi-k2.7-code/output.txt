## Accessibility notes

The main readability risk is the **gray body text on the near-black background** in both the hero and feature sections. Depending on the exact gray value, it may fall below WCAG AA contrast thresholds for normal-sized text (4.5:1). The large white H1 on black is excellent. The white button labels also appear to have sufficient contrast, but the secondary/outline “Read the docs” button and any future small card text should be checked with a contrast checker.

## Summary

- **Verify and, if needed, darken the gray body copy** in the hero and feature sections to hit WCAG AA contrast for normal text.
- **Audit the eventual H3/card headings** for size/weight differentiation once the card titles are added, so the hierarchy does not flatten.
- **Confirm small text contrast** on the dark cards and secondary buttons before launch.
