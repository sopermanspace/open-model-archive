Here are the solutions to the math problems, with step-by-step reasoning for each:

**Problem 1**
*   Maya starts with 24 stickers.
*   She gives 7 away: 24 - 7 = 17 stickers.
*   She buys 15 more: 17 + 15 = 32 stickers.
*   **Final answer:** 32 stickers

**Problem 2**
*   The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is 2 × (length + width).
*   Add the length and width: 12 + 5 = 17 meters.
*   Multiply by 2: 2 × 17 = 34 meters.
*   **Final answer:** 34 meters

**Problem 3**
*   First, find the total number of slices by multiplying the number of pizzas by the slices per pizza: 3 pizzas × 8 slices/pizza = 24 total slices.
*   Next, divide the total slices by the number of friends: 24 slices ÷ 4 friends = 6 slices per friend.
*   **Final answer:** 6 slices

**Problem 4**
*   Calculate the cost of the notebooks: 4 notebooks × $2 = $8.
*   Calculate the cost of the pens: 6 pens × $1 = $6.
*   Find the total cost: $8 + $6 = $14.
*   Subtract the total cost from the amount paid to find the change: $20 - $14 = $6.
*   **Final answer:** $6

**Problem 5**
*   First, calculate the train's speed by dividing the distance by the time: 60 miles ÷ 1.5 hours = 40 miles per hour.
*   Next, multiply this speed by the new time to find the new distance: 40 miles/hour × 4 hours = 160 miles.
*   **Final answer:** 160 miles
