How Drift Works

Real-Time Comparison

Don't wait for manual audits. Our system constantly monitors resource definitions and environment states, alerting you the second a deviation occurs.

Support for AWS CloudFormation, Kubernetes manifests, Terraform state files, and custom configuration parameters—all under one dashboard.

Detailed, tamper-proof logs showing *who*, *when*, and *how* a drift occurred. Includes one-click remediation suggestions to bring environments back into compliance.
