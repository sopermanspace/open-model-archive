Our Ethos

Gather around
the hearth.

Born from the embers of the Pacific Northwest, our kitchen is a tribute to the elemental art of cooking with fire. We partner with local foragers and heritage grain farmers to craft a dining experience that is both rugged and refined.

Every evening, the dining room glows with the warmth of our open hearth. We believe in the slow transformation of ingredients—wood smoke infusing root vegetables, meats slowly roasting over oak coals, and grains fermented naturally over days. It is a return to cooking in its purest form, stripped of pretense, anchored by the seasons.
