From Fire to Feast

Our ethos begins with the woods and ends at your table. At Ember & Oak, we honor the primal connection between earth and flame. Our ingredients are sourced within a hundred-mile radius of Portland—from heritage grain flour milled in Oregon to forest mushrooms harvested by local foragers. The cooking method is deliberately rustic: time-honored wood fire techniques meeting modern finesse.

"The perfect meal is less about plated perfection and more about the collective memory of a shared, slow moment. We build our dining room around that stillness."
— Chef Rowan Grey
