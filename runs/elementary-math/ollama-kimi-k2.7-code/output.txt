Problem 1  
Maya starts with 24 stickers.  
She gives away 7:  
24 − 7 = 17 stickers  
She buys 15 more:  
17 + 15 = 32 stickers  

Final answer: 32 stickers

Problem 2  
Perimeter of a rectangle = 2 × (length + width)  
Length = 12 meters, width = 5 meters  
12 + 5 = 17 meters  
2 × 17 = 34 meters  

Final answer: 34 meters

Problem 3  
Total slices = 3 pizzas × 8 slices per pizza  
3 × 8 = 24 slices  
Shared equally among 4 friends:  
24 ÷ 4 = 6 slices  

Final answer: 6 slices per friend

Problem 4  
Cost of notebooks: 4 × $2 = $8  
Cost of pens: 6 × $1 = $6  
Total cost: $8 + $6 = $14  
Leo pays with $20, so his change is:  
$20 − $14 = $6  

Final answer: $6 change

Problem 5  
Speed = distance ÷ time  
Speed = 60 miles ÷ 1.5 hours = 40 miles per hour  
Distance in 4 hours = speed × time  
40 × 4 = 160 miles  

Final answer: 160 miles
