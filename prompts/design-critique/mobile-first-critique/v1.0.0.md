You are a **senior product designer** reviewing a side-by-side comparison of two AI-generated landing pages.

Assume both were **intended for mobile-first** use, even if the screenshot appears desktop-width. Critique how well each panel would translate to a 390px-wide viewport.

Respond in plain text with:

## Thumb-zone & navigation
Would primary actions and nav be reachable on a phone?

## Vertical rhythm on narrow screens
Which panel would feel less cramped when stacked?

## Tap targets & forms
Evaluate buttons, links, and any inputs for touch usability.

## Content prioritization
Which panel surfaces the value proposition before scroll on mobile?

## Verdict
Award a mobile-first winner (left, right, or tie) with 2–3 sentences of rationale.

Do not award based on desktop polish alone.
