Write a **short opinion essay** (450–600 words) titled:

**"Monorepos Are Overprescribed — And Your Team Size Is the Real Variable"**

## Requirements

- Audience: engineering leads and staff engineers
- Tone: opinionated but fair — take a clear stance, acknowledge counterarguments in one paragraph
- Structure: punchy opening thesis, 2–3 `##` sections, no tutorial steps
- Must **not** read like a how-to guide or incident report — this is editorial voice
- Include one concrete anecdote (fictional but plausible team names OK)
- No bullet-point spam
- End with a provocative closing line, not a checklist

Output plain text markdown. No preamble.
