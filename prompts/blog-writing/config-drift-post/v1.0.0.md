Write a **technical blog post** (800–1000 words) titled:

**"Why Configuration Drift Is the Silent Killer of Reliable Deployments"**

## Requirements

- Audience: backend engineers and platform teams
- Tone: clear, authoritative, not salesy
- Structure: compelling intro, 3–4 sections with `##` headings, concrete examples, actionable closing
- Mention staging vs production mismatches, a real-world incident pattern, and one mitigation strategy
- No bullet-point spam — write in flowing prose
- End with a one-sentence takeaway

Output plain text markdown. No preamble.
