## Source excerpts

**[Source A — Remote work policy]**
Full-time employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the employee's local timezone. Remote work beyond 3 days requires director approval.

**[Source B — Equipment stipend]**
New hires receive a one-time $750 home office stipend in their first payroll cycle. Existing employees are eligible for a $300 annual refresh stipend after 12 months. Stipends do not roll over.

**[Source C — Travel & offsites]**
The company hosts one mandatory annual team offsite. Travel is booked by HR. Employees who cannot attend for medical reasons must notify HR 30 days in advance with documentation.
