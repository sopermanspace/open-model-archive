You are reviewing a **landing page screenshot** attached to this task.

Perform a **typography and readability audit only**. Do not critique color palette, photography, or conversion funnels unless they directly affect reading.

Respond in plain text with these sections:

## Type scale & hierarchy
Are heading levels distinguishable? Is there a clear H1 → body progression?

## Font pairing & voice
Do the typefaces feel intentional? Any generic or mismatched choices?

## Line length & density
Is body text comfortable to read? Any cramped or overly wide blocks?

## Microtypography
Evaluate button labels, nav links, and small print for legibility and casing consistency.

## Accessibility notes
Contrast issues that affect text readability specifically.

## Summary
Three bullet priorities a designer should fix first (typography only).

Be specific. Reference regions of the page (hero, nav, footer, etc.).
