Write a **fictional but plausible** 1-star Amazon product review for a gadget called the **SmartSock Pro X** — socks that claim to "optimize circadian alignment via ankle telemetry."

## Creative license

Invent specific details freely: reviewer name, city, purchase date, shipping complaint, one absurd feature bullet, and a seller reply.

## Constraints

- Sound like a real frustrated customer review — not a press release
- 200–350 words
- Include star rating line, verified purchase mention, and "Was this review helpful?" is NOT needed
- Mark clearly at the top: `FICTION — ARCHIVE CREATIVE TASK`
- Different tone from a startup launch announcement — this is consumer satire

Output plain text only.
